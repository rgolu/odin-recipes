Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is favourate dish of many. It has blend of different taste and spices and looks fabulous.It is made from chicken,Rice,ghee and alot of spices.There are are variety of variation of chicken biryani like hydrabadi biryani,kolkata biryani,Moradabadi biryani etc.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Take half kg chicken.Make few slits on all chicken pieces.
  2. Mix Well and marinate the chicken.Cover and set this aside for 1 hr.
  3. Soak Basmati Rice in fresh water for 30min
  4. Heat ghee or Oil in a heavy pot or pressure cooker
  5. Add thinly sliced onions.On a medium heat,fry them stirring often and until uniformly light brown
  6. Add marinated chicken and salute it becomes pale for 5 min
  7. Lower the flame completely. cover and cook till chicken is soft,tender and completely cooked
  8. When the pressure releases naturally, open the lid.Gently fluff up with a fork.

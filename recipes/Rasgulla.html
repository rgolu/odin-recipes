Rasgulla

Description

Rasgulla is one of the popular Indian sweet recipes that is made by curding milk. Then separating the chenna(paneer or indian cottage cheese) and whey by draining in a nuslin cloth. The drained chenna is kneaded and then rolled and then rolled to balls. These are cooked in sugar syrup till they turn light and spongy.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • sweet Syrup

Steps

  1. first rinse heavy bottom utensil to boil milk.By rinsing,you can prevent the milk from being burnt beneath.Pour milk and bring it to a boil over a medium big heat. Keep stirring to prevent a layer of cream forming on top.I used Homogenized milk.
  2. Continue stirring and add lemon juice and turn off stove.
  3. Add Ice cubes and rest for 2 min.
  4. wrap the chenna in a cloth and rinse well under running water.
  5. knead the chenna and make small dough balls.
  6. Add sugar syrup in a pot and heat it to rolling boil.
  7. Add the balls to the syrup
  8. Keep the pan covered and boil for 9 to 10 min.
